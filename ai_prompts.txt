# AI Prompt Library – Ready-to-Use Templates

## 1. Professional Marketing Email (Tested with ChatGPT/Claude)
Act as a professional email copywriter for a modern, community-focused coffee shop called "Brew Haven" in DeSoto, TX.

Write a short, engaging marketing email announcing our grand opening next Saturday.

Include:
- Warm, friendly tone
- Special offer: 20% off first visit with code "WELCOME20"
- Call to action: Visit us at 123 Main St
- Under 150 words
- Bullet points for the offer
- Fun sign-off like "See you soon!"

## 2. Social Media Post Series (Instagram/TikTok)
You are a creative social media manager for a local business.

Generate 5 short, engaging Instagram caption ideas for a new coffee shop opening. Each should:
- Highlight a unique feature (e.g., artisanal drinks, cozy vibe, local art)
- Include relevant emojis
- End with a call-to-action (tag a friend, visit us)
- Use hashtags: #DeSotoTX #LocalCoffee #BrewHaven

## 3. Product Description Writer (E-commerce)
Act as an expert e-commerce copywriter.

Write a compelling 100-word product description for a "Signature Latte Kit" home brewing set. Emphasize:
- Premium ingredients
- Easy to use at home
- Café-quality taste
- Perfect gift idea
- Tone: Warm, aspirational, trustworthy

## 4. Customer Support Response Template
You are a friendly customer support agent for a small online store.

Draft a polite, professional reply to a customer complaining about a late delivery. Include:
- Empathy and apology
- Explanation (weather delays)
- Solution (refund or discount)
- Positive closing

## 5. Blog Outline Generator
Act as a content strategist.

Create a detailed outline for a 1500-word blog post titled "10 Hidden Gems in DeSoto, Texas for Coffee Lovers". Include:
- Introduction hook
- Numbered list with 1–2 sentences per gem
- Conclusion with CTA to visit Brew Haven
